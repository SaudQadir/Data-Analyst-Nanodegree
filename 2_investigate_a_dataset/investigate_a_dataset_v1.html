Conclusions¶

  • Movies with greater budget and revenue gets more vote average. The reason could be that greater budget in movies relates with more advertisement and promotion of the movie thus leading to more views and more vote counts and more vote average
  • Movies having runtime greater than 150 minutes always gets average more than 5. But there might be other reason behind it such a those movies are produced by big production team and shorter length movies produced by a small production company
